Our Process

How we do, What we do.

1. Initial Assessment

We start by inspecting your windows to identify specific cleaning needs, such as dirt buildup, water stains, or any potential issues that may need attention.

2. Protecting Your Space

Before we begin, we take precautions to protect your property. We place drop cloths around the work area and move any nearby furniture or items to prevent damage.

3. Pre-Cleaning Preparation

We remove any screens and brush away loose dirt, cobwebs, and debris around the windows and frames to ensure a clean working surface.
