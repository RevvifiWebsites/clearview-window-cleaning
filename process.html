Our Process

Our Process

How we do, What we do.

1. Initial Assessment

We start by inspecting your windows to identify specific cleaning needs, such as dirt buildup, water stains, or any potential issues that may need attention.

2. Protecting Your Space

Before we begin, we take precautions to protect your property. We place drop cloths around the work area and move any nearby furniture or items to prevent damage.

3. Pre-Cleaning Preparation

We remove any screens and brush away loose dirt, cobwebs, and debris around the windows and frames to ensure a clean working surface.

4. Deep Cleaning

Using eco-friendly cleaning solutions and specialized tools, we clean both the interior and exterior of your windows. We pay special attention to the frames, sills, and tracks for a comprehensive finish.

5. Detailing and Polishing

Once the windows are cleaned, we squeegee them dry to remove water spots and streaks. Our team also wipes down the edges, frames, and any remaining moisture for a crystal-clear appearance.

6. Final Inspection

We conduct a final check to ensure every window meets our high standards. If needed, we provide touch-ups to guarantee your complete satisfaction.

7. Post-Service Cleanup

After the job is complete, we reinstall screens, tidy up the workspace, and leave your home or business just as clean as we found it — with sparkling windows as the only trace of our visit!
